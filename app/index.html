About Myself

I've worked as a Content Editor Manager for several years at Franklin University. I’ve edited and implemented hundreds of online courses, working as a team of instructional designers and subject matter experts. I’ve managed a team of five editors, as well as publication deadlines and design schedules. I’ve developed numerous Articulate Storyline training pieces for courses and for clients, and have designed and developed print programs and materials for University conferences.

Outside of Franklin University, I’ve edited novels, dissertations, and scholarly journal articles, all of which have made it to publication. I love using words to convey thoughts and ideas, and for these words to effectively communication with the intended audience.

For fun, I love reading young adult dystopian fiction, spending time with my family, and exploring Columbus.
